Fun Projects

Light Up

In this calming Thadingyut game, players gently light a candle and an oil lamp. There’s no rush or goal—just enjoy the peaceful act of lighting them and watching the soft glow fill the scene, creating a serene atmosphere for the festival.

Lantern

In "Thadingyut Lantern Launch," players release a single glowing lantern into the night sky to celebrate the beauty of Thadingyut. As you let the lantern float upwards, watch it gently drift among the stars, lighting up the peaceful night.

Firework

In "Thadingyut Firework Show," players get to select and launch five different fireworks to create a dazzling display in the night sky. Enjoy the beauty and excitement as each firework paints the sky with brilliant colors.
